Assistant Professor

I joined as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Management Studies and Industrial Engineering at IIT(ISM) Dhanbad, in April 2023. Before that, I was affiliated with DIME, IIT Kanpur, as Postdoctoral Researcher. I did Ph.D. from Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi, in 2021. My research interests are Asset Pricing, Volatility Modelling, Clean Energy Finance, Sustainability, and Text Analytics in Finance.
